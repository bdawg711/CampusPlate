Privacy Policy

CampusPlate — Last updated: March 2026

Introduction

CampusPlate ("we," "our," or "the app") is a nutrition tracking application designed for Virginia Tech students. We take your privacy seriously. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

Information We Collect

When you use CampusPlate, we collect the following information:

  • Account information: Email address and password (managed securely through Supabase Auth).
  • Profile data: Name, age, height, weight, gender, activity level, and dietary preferences you provide during onboarding or in settings.
  • Nutrition data: Meals you log, custom meals you create, water intake, weight entries, and calculated nutrition totals.
  • Usage data: Dining hall ratings, reviews, favorites, streak data, and AI chat conversations within the app.
  • Device information: Push notification tokens for delivering reminders you enable.

Information We Do Not Collect

  • We do not collect your location data.
  • We do not access your contacts, photos, camera, or microphone.
  • We do not use advertising trackers or sell your data to third parties.
  • We do not collect data from users under 13 years of age.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely to provide and improve the CampusPlate experience:

  • To calculate and display your daily nutrition totals, scores, streaks, and progress charts.
  • To power the AI meal recommendation feature using your nutrition goals and today's dining hall menus.
  • To generate weekly nutrition reports.
  • To send push notification reminders you have opted into.
  • To display dining hall ratings and reviews from the community.

AI Features

CampusPlate includes an AI meal planning assistant. When you use this feature, your nutrition goals and today's menu data are sent to Anthropic's Claude API to generate meal recommendations. Your conversations are stored in the app's database so you can view your chat history. We do not share your personal information with Anthropic beyond what is needed to generate a response.

Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Supabase, a hosted database platform with industry-standard encryption. All data is transmitted over HTTPS. Row-level security (RLS) is enabled on all database tables, ensuring that each user can only access their own data. We do not store your password in plain text.

Dining Hall Data

CampusPlate displays publicly available menu and nutrition information from Virginia Tech's dining services. This data is refreshed daily and includes menu items, nutrition facts, ingredients, and dining hall operating hours.

Third-Party Services

CampusPlate uses the following third-party services:

  • Supabase: Database hosting and user authentication. Supabase Privacy Policy
  • Anthropic (Claude API): AI meal recommendations. Anthropic Privacy Policy
  • Expo / EAS: App build and push notification delivery. Expo Privacy Policy
  • Apple App Store: App distribution. Apple Privacy Policy

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data: All your nutrition data, logs, and profile information are visible within the app.
  • Delete your data: You can delete individual meal logs, custom meals, and weight entries from within the app. To request full account deletion, contact us at the email below.
  • Opt out of notifications: You can disable any push notification from the Settings screen at any time.

Children's Privacy

CampusPlate is designed for college students and is not intended for use by children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected data from a child under 13, we will delete it promptly.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
